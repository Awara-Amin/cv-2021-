History of The Church

The entire Outer Hebrides is underlined by Archean Lewisian gneisses (the Lewisian complex). It is recognized as the oldest rocks exposed in Scotland and Britain. Here, we focus on the thermal evolution of these rocks after they reach close to the surface (3-5 km depth) at around 250 Ma years ago. For that, AFT and AHe analysis are employed on the Lewisian complex rocks collected across the Outer Hebrides.
